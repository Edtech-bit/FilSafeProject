OUR STORY

Built on Trust, Driven by Innovation

Our the company was established last April 6 2002, FILSAFE ENTERPRISES counts on the wealth of expertise of its staff and personnel gained through decades of experience in the security equipment field both here in the Philippines and abroad; experience gained through years of exposure to all phases of Production, Engineering and Design, and Customer Service.

The Company envisages in providing high quality products and services at reasonable prices and to actively participate and play a major role towards the establishment of a Security Equipment Manufacturing Standard for the Philippines. Our vision of providing the right product for the need of our clientele remains our primary goal and we are totally committed towards this end.

"Security is not just about products, it's about trust, expertise, and commitment. At Filsafe, we protect what matters most to you, 24/7."
